When was pizza first introduced in the Philippines?

Pizza arrived on Philippine waters, thanks in part to our American colonists. Greenwich, a popular local pizza business, debuted in 1971, followed by American brands Shakey's and Pizza Hut in 1975 and 1984, as well. (Source: Pizza: Guide, History, Best Manila Restos | SPOT.ph )
